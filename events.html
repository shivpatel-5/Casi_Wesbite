Case Competition

Theme

Near-future space systems (TRL 3–6)

Teams act as a multidisciplinary aerospace consultancy proposing a technically feasible solution under real constraints (risk, cost, sustainability, ops).

The participants will be shared the case competition problem statement in advance to prepare their solutions, which will be judged by a panel of industry experts on the second day.
