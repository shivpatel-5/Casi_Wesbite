Beyond Earth: The Next Frontier

Sat, Mar 14 & Sun, Mar 15 | Daphne Cockwell Centre (DCC) 103/104

A two-day CASI TMU winter conference featuring keynotes, panels, networking, and a case competition focused on near-future mission challenges. Theme: Sustainability in Engineering for extreme and autonomous space operations.
