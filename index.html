Our Mission and Vision

Who We Are

The CASI TMU serves as a hub for the Canadian Aeronautics and Space Institute (CASI) TMU Chapter, where we aim to connect students with aerospace events, industry leaders, and professional development opportunities. Our organization is committed to fostering a collaborative environment that inspires and nurtures the next generation of aerospace professionals, enhancing their educational experiences and career paths.
